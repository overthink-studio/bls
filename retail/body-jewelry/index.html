27 Important Qualities That You Want in Your New Body Jewellery

Body ornamentation is one of the oldest and most effective ways to express yourself. That includes investing in body jewellery. If this is something you’ve been thinking about but aren’t sure how to tackle, it pays to know what to look for in the right selections. Here are several key qualities that must be present.

Only the Best Materials Will Do

There are plenty of choices for body jewellery out there and not all of them are good. One of the first things you want to look for is the use of quality materials. It’s not just about durability, although that is important. You want quality because the pieces will look new for a longer period of time and you won’t have any problems due to the quality of the metal. They’ll also look better than their cheap counterparts from the onset.

What materials do you want? One option is to stick with something like 14k gold piercing jewellery. You can also look into the use of surgical steel or possibly titanium. While you will spend more for these kinds of metals, the benefits far outweigh the costs.

Designs That Appeal to Your Taste

A piece may look nice, but that’s not enough. The design has to be in line with your personal tastes. Since the point is self-expression, there’s no reason to buy something that doesn’t fit in with your personality and the image that you want to project.

When you head out to look at options for gold nose piercing jewelry, do have some idea of themes that would make a design appealing to you. Also consider if the piercing is one you will wear daily or may only wear for special occasions. Through it all, make sure you like the way the piece looks.

Never hesitate to consider looking into custom designs. A talented professional can take the ideas in your head and give them expression in a specially designed piece.

And Happen to Be Out of the Ordinary

You’ll quickly find that there’s a world of ho-hum gold plated piercing jewelry on the market. Some of the designs are so benign that they would not do anything for you at all. Those are pieces that you don’t want to waste time considering. What you need is something that’s a little out of the ordinary.

Look for pieces that have some feature that’s not often found with the rest. It could be the type or the arrangement of jewels in the design. Maybe it’s the shape or the dimensions. As long as it strikes you as being something that doesn’t look just like what others are wearing and it appeals to you, that piece is worth considering.

Quality Crafting Matters Too

Imagination and quality materials matter a great deal. If you really want the jewellery to work, then the craftsmanship must be there as well. Some of the best design ideas in the world can easily be ruined when the crafting is below par.

For this reason, always pay close attention to the way the piece is crafted. It should be sturdy, free of imperfections that take away from the design, and obviously exude the superior crafting that you deserve. Anything less is not worth your time or money.

As Does Embellishments That Add Visual Appeal

One of the wonderful things about piercing jewellery is that it can be as simple or as ornate as one wants. In your case, adding some extra touches would make the piece more to your liking. Precious stones paired with the right metals can make quite a statement.

Think about what you would like with a piece of rose gold daith piercing jewelry. Would a stone or two worked into the design of this nose jewellery provide the look that you want? Perhaps a dash of colour added to part of the jewellery while the bare metal is left exposed elsewhere? If you can imagine it, there’s a designer who can make it happen.

Size Counts

Proportion is also something to consider when it comes to any type of body jewellery. While that’s subjective up to a point, there are some basics to keep in mind. The piece should not be so large that it interferes with movement or function. At the same time, it should not be so small that even you have trouble noticing that it’s present.

If you’re not sure how to opt for something that’s the right size, do ask for help from one of our professionals. It doesn’t take long to consider three or more sizes and find what seems to be the ideal balance.

Practical Matters: A Protective Finish

Along with the appearance, the design, and the choice of metals, do consider the nature of the finish that’s applied to the rose gold piercing. It should protect the appearance, but it should also be a finish that does not irritate the skin. That’s important in any case, but it’s especially crucial when the piercing happens to be in a more intimate location.

Choose your new body jewellery with care. Remember that the pieces selected should be high enough in quality to last for a number of years and that you should find the designs to your liking. Doing so provides the perfect balance between practicality, fun, and appearance.
